Unit 1, Outcome 1 1.1 Describing the purpose of IT in modern business

Computer technology is used throughout the business world by all departments and has become essential to the business operations of the modern world.[1]
Companies are also leveraging IT for a variety of contexts and purposes.
Therefore, IT is an essential technology in modern society because it improves business communication, increases efficiency, protects critical assets, finds new resources to improve business, and saves time and money.[2]
Therefore, the purpose of IT are productivity improvement, profit increase, technology enhancement, information acquisition and protection, information sharing among members, company promotion and marketing strategy and so on.

Firstly, unrealistic time management will not keep up with the rapidly changing IT environment, because the IT environment changes so fast nowadays.
Secondly, unplanned project will lead us in a completely different direction. So we have to make a thorough plan and check one by one to complete the goal step by step.

Unit 1, Outcome 1 1.7 Describe legal guidelines and constraints that impact digital projects – How does GDPR impact digital projects?

GDPR regulation replaces the Data Protection Directive (1995) and is set to have far-reaching impacts on individuals and organisations in all sectors globally.1) The purpose of GDPR is to protect individual data.
According to GDPR, individuals also have the right to access the data you have.
These include when, how, and what they agreed to, and the data you have.[1]
The most important area where GDPR has a huge impact on digital/IT industries is the data processing area. we can never use any data without permission. Also this regulation includes a very heavy fine. Thus IT worker or digital marketers can never ignore GDPR.
